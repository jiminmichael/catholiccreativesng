Catholic Creatives Hub Nigeria Community Membership Questionnaire

Catholic Creatives Nigeria, is a platform birthed out of the need to build a community of Catholic Creatives, who will be devoted to using their God-given gifts and talents to create contents that will enthrone and uphold catholic values, leveraging on the creative and entertainment industry to push Catholic contents. The hallmark of this platform is community; bringing catholic creatives together and building a support system for them by creating the much-needed opportunities for collaboration among themselves, providing mentorship from established creatives, achieving independence, and earning a living from their creative works. Our goal is to profile young creative, gifted, and talented Catholics, mentor and equip them to actualize and utilize their God-given gifts and potentials, achieve independence, and become resourceful, thereby, impacting positively on their local community and the larger society.
